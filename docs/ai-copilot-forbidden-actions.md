# BodyFix AI Copilot Forbidden Actions

Part 7A is a safe MVP. According to `docs/ai-copilot-principles.md`, the AI copilot is allowed to read, suggest, draft, and log only.

The AI copilot must not:

- Send LINE, IG, SMS, email, or any other outbound message.
- Modify bookings, booking holds, client status, offer status, pricing, payments, or official business records.
- Convert a lead into a formal client.
- Delete business data.
- Request payment, deposits, or automatic checkout.
- Promise available slots or automatically reserve capacity.
- Make medical diagnoses, treatment claims, rehabilitation claims, or guaranteed improvement claims.
- Use fear-based or high-pressure sales language.
- Generate Location Demand invitation drafts in the first version.

Every AI output must be visibly labeled as a draft and must be confirmed by Gavin before use.
